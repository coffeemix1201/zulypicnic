FAQs

    Reservations

  • We prefer 2 weeks in advance but we can work within 72 hrs notice.

    Reservation Deposit

  • There is a $125 deposit at the time of the reservation. The remaining balance is due 10 days before the event.

    Cancellation

  • If you need to cancel your event, you will need to cancel 10 days in advance in order to receive a full refund of your deposit. After 10 days reservation deposit will not be refunded.

    Location

  • We will email you different locations that we offer but if you have a place in mind, we can make it happen.

    Bad Weather

  • If the weather takes a turn for the worst and we need to postpone, we will allow you 2-3 days in advance to cancel and postpone the event date. (This option is only available twice).
